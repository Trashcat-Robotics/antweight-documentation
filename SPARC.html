1.10 SPARC-0011

The platform shall limit spinning devices (rotating more than 360 degrees) to a tip speed of no more than 6 m/s (20 ft/s), unless:

  • The spinning device is a saw with an effective tooth pitch of >= 5.
  • Effective tooth pitch = tooth count / diameter.
  • In the case of an abrasive saw, the tooth count = grit*circumference
